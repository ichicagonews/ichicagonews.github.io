About the Platform

iChicagoNews is a bilingual (Chinese-English) digital media platform based in Chicago. We provide high-quality content to overseas Chinese communities and international students in the Midwest. Our official WeChat account @ichicagonews has over 16,000 followers, and we manage active WeChat groups of more than 20,000 members across student, professional, and lifestyle categories.

Platform Reach & Demographics

Among our 16,000+ followers, 51.14% are female and 48.76% are male. The largest age groups are 46–60, 36–45, and 26–35. Most of our readers are based in Chicago, and many come from major cities in China such as Beijing and Shanghai.

Popular Content

Our posts have gone viral in the Chicago Chinese community, with some articles receiving more than 10,000 views. We are recognized for our fast, practical, and engaging local reporting.
